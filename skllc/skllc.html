The Process:


Step 1 - Consultation:

Initial meeting where we discuss the origin story and future vision of your small business! What problem can we solve together? Project begins!



Step 2 - Gathering Requirements:

Clearly lay out the landing page, images, art style, testimony, about info, feature list, estimate, contract and other general information along with a blueprint for the project as a whole!



Step 3 - Graphic Design:

Any physical or digital art commissioned for the project is created by hand or digitally and added to the project files to make the website beautiful and unique!



Step 4 - Development:

Software solution development begins, several rounds of agile meetings are performed in sync with development. Changes and features are finalized and approved by the business owners!



Step 5 - Software Solution Deployment:

The software solution is deployed live on the internet! Follow up meeting to evaluate customer satisfaction, quality of work, and future aspirations!



Step 6 - Content Management and Updates:

As business partners we continue to progress, managing content changes, additions, and setting up a CMS (Content Management System) so the client can be involved in the creativite process!



Step 7 - Results:

The problem we identified in the beginning of the project is solved! We can celebrate our vision becoming tangible and meeting our goals together!
