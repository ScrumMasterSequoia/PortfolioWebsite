Frequently Asked Questions:

1. Q: How much does a basic website cost to build and maintain?

A: A simple site should cost about two thousand dollars, however there are many variables, such as graphic design, feature requirements, and maintenance costs. As a general policy, we are open minded to offering an up-front discount if the client opts for maintenance. For minimalistic SPA (single page applications) we are willing to negotiate prices as low as $500 or free up-front, plus maintenance

2. Q: How long does it take to build a website??

A: Depending on the complexity, it can take anywhere from two weeks to three months after all starting requirements have been met. Complex projects that require Database and Security take significantly longer and cost more.

3. Q: How much does it cost to add graphic design, logo and slogan?

A: We prefer the customer to create the slogan, as it is their company but we are happy to assist. Depending on scope and complexity, each digital art piece will cost between $50 and $200.

4. Q: What other services do you offer, beyond website creation?

A: We offer professional consulting, which includes technical and business consulting, physical art, graphic design, social media packages, Search Engine Optimization and more! For a full list, see Services

5. Q: When, how often and how do I handle changing requirements after the initial contract is signed?

A: We have built into our development stage that there are multiple meetings alongside development, which account for free changes to requirements. Beyond that, changes to requirement will be handled on a case-by-case basis and will often result in additional costs and additional time to software solution delivery.
